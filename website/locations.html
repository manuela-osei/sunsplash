FESTIVAL LOCATION

Sunsplash Music Festival takes place in Mowsbury Park, Bedford.

The festival site will be in the north end of Mowsbury Park itself. A map of the festival site, showing where everything is, will be published on the website and available on the festival app nearer the time. It would also be distributed at the entrance.

Address: Bedford Mk41 8QA Incase of any misdirections, you can contact us for accurate description.

Contacts:
0207174706 or 0200727654

Opening Hour: 12:00pm

The park would be opened from 12:00 but the music event starts at 2.00 pm

TRANSPORTATION

National Rail Services are available. Train can be taken to Bedford station. From the station, it takes 10 minutes by car, 56 minutes by foot, 16 minutes by car and 23 minutes or more by bus to Mowsbury Park. There will be a cab rank situated at the BedforddStation, which will be outside of the train station.
